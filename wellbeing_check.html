Health and Wellbeing Check

Details of medical assessment

  • Review of Medical History
  • Height
  • Weight
  • Blood Pressure
  • Heart Rate
  • Oxygen Saturation
  • Vision Test

Documents required to bring at assessment

  • ID Document
  • D4 Form
  • Prescription for glasses (if you wear glasses for driving)
  • List of medicines (if you take any medication)
  • Three blood pressure readings from the last week (if you take medication for blood pressure)

Note:Please bring your glasses with you, if you wear glasses for driving.
